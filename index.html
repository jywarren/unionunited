5 THINGS

you should know about
Union Square's future

  1. Plans are to house 1-2,000 new people, with over 80% un-afforable housing units
  2. Rents are expected to rise 20-25%, or by over $250 per bedroom.1
  3. ~1500 households are expected to be displaced along the Green Line extension.2
  4. Only X% of new housing will be available for households making <$Y.
  5. Thousands of Somerville residents are waiting over a year each for affordable housing (real #s)
